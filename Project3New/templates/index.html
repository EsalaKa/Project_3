Exploring Global Intelligence Metrics

This web application serves as a comprehensive resource for accessing a range of important country-specific information. It is designed to provide useful data about various countries, including:

  • Average IQ: Understand the average intelligence levels.
  • Literacy Percentage: Explore literacy rates across different nations.
  • Nobel Prize Winners: Discover the cumulative count of Nobel Prize associated with each country.
  • Average School Education: Average duration of schooling.
  • Human Development Index (HDI): Assess the overall development and standard of living.
  • Population: Updated population of countries.
When a country is selected from the dropdown menu, the application displays the above mentioned details, offering an in-depth look at each country's characteristics. Additionally, by interacting with the map, users can visually identify the IQ levels of different regions. This feature effectively illustrates geographical variations, highlighting areas with generally high, medium, or lower IQ scores.

Furthermore, the application provides scatter plot visualizations that depict the correlation between IQ and literacy (Very low positive correlation), as well as the relationship between education levels and the number of Nobel Prize winners (No identified correlation). These correlations offer a unique perspective on how different educational and intellectual metrics interact globally.
